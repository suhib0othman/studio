# **App Name**: AI Assist Pro

## Core Features:

- Personalized Assessment Flow: A multi-step, card-based assessment wizard with a progress bar and auto-save, guiding users through 10 questions to gather data for AI analysis.
- AI-Powered Opportunity Generation: An advanced AI tool that analyzes user assessment responses to generate a highly personalized report, including an 'AI Wealth Score,' profile summary, top 5 tailored income opportunities with detailed metrics (e.g., compatibility, earning potential, startup cost, recommended AI tools), and an achievement badge.
- Interactive Results Dashboard: A premium dashboard experience to display the comprehensive AI-generated report, allowing users to view their wealth score, explore detailed opportunity cards, and access in-depth 'Full Plan' modals for each recommended pathway.
- Social Share Card Generator: Automatically generates a personalized, high-quality image summarizing the user's key results (e.g., AI Wealth Score, top opportunities, achievement badge) for easy sharing across social media platforms.
- Secure User Authentication & Profile: Enables users to create accounts, securely log in, and manage their personal profiles, assessment data, and saved AI-generated reports.
- Result Persistence & History: A system to save user assessments and their corresponding AI-generated results, allowing users to revisit, review, and track their personalized income roadmaps over time.
- Referral System: Generates unique referral links, tracks invites, registrations, and completed assessments, rewarding users for inviting friends to the platform.

## Style Guidelines:

- Theme: Premium Dark Mode. Background: #0B1220 (A deep, rich dark background that sets a premium, sophisticated tone).
- Primary: #4F46E5 (A vibrant indigo primary for interactive elements and brand accents, conveying innovation).
- Secondary: #7C3AED (A luminous purple-magenta secondary for subtle gradients and emphasis, complementing the primary).
- Accent: #06B6D4 (A striking teal accent used sparingly for calls to action and critical highlights, ensuring high visibility).
- Text: #FFFFFF (Pure white for primary text, ensuring maximum readability against the dark background). Muted Text: #94A3B8 (A soft gray for secondary information and subdued elements, maintaining a sleek contrast). Success: #10B981 (A distinct green for success messages and positive indicators).
- Body and Headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern, neutral, and highly readable appearance, used with large bold headings and professional spacing to match a premium SaaS aesthetic.
- Sleek, minimalist vector icons with subtle gradients or glassmorphism effects, consistent with the premium design language and dark mode interface.
- A responsive, mobile-first design featuring glassmorphism cards with gradient borders and soft shadows, creating a sense of depth and 'floating' elements on the dark background for a truly premium SaaS feel, including modern charts and beautiful score indicators.
- Subtle and smooth UI animations and transitions for navigation, form interactions, floating elements, and data loading states (especially during the AI analysis process), enhancing the perception of a fluid and high-end application.
